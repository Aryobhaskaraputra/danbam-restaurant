About DanBam Restaurant

Itaewon Class tells the story of former convict Park Sae-roy (Park Seo-joon) whose life has been turned upside down after he was expelled from school for punching a bully, and his father was killed in an accident. Following in his father's footsteps, he opened the restaurant bar DanBam in Itaewon and together with his manager and staff, strives towards success and reaching higher levels.

DanBam is a restaurant that is going viral in Korea. Currently, DanBam has several branches all over Korea. DanBam Restaurant offers a menu of traditional Korean food which is packaged in a modern way
